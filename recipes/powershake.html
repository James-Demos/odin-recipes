Powershake

Description

This protein shake is easy, fast, and packs a punch with 50 grams of protein, a serving of creatine, spinach, and a reasonable amount of peanut butter for fat and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 63 g Whey protein powder
  • 6-8 g creatine monohydrate
  • 25 g spinach
  • 16 g peanut butter
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 1 cup cold water

Steps

  1. add the powders first to create a bed for the peanut butter
  2. add peanut butter
  3. add spinach. Be sure to cover peanut butter fully
  4. add ice
  5. add water>
  6. blend until smooth
  7. Wash your carafe right away, we're not cavemen.
  8. drink it.
