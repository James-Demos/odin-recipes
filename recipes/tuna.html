Tuna Steaks

Description

This is an excellent red meat or poultry alternative for your protein fix. Super fast and delicious.

Note: this is meant to be at most medium rare. Do not overcook. You'll be fine.

Ingredients

  • Any sexy tuna steak you can find
  • olive oil
  • soy sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. bring heat on frying pan up to medium-high
  2. pour in any reasonable amount of olive oil
  3. add the steak and cook on one side for only 2 minutes
  4. flip and cook on other side for 2 minutes
  5. remove steak from heat and place into shallow bowl of soy sauce
  6. add salt pepper and sesame seeds liberally
  7. get after it
